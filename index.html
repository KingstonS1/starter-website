Tote Bag DIY

Learn how to sew, iron, and screen print your own tote bag!

Sewing

Here are the basic steps for sewing a tote bag:

  1. Choose your fabric and pattern.
  2. Cut the fabric according to the pattern.
  3. Sew the sides and bottom of the bag.
  4. Attach handles to the bag.
  5. Optional: Add pockets or embellishments.

Ironing

Proper ironing is essential for a neat tote bag:

  • Iron the fabric before cutting to remove wrinkles.
  • Iron seams flat for a professional finish.
  • Use a pressing cloth to protect delicate fabrics.
  • Adjust iron temperature based on fabric type.

Screen Printing

Screen printing adds a personal touch to your tote bag:

  1. Create or choose a design for your tote bag.
  2. Transfer the design onto a screen.
  3. Place the screen on the fabric and apply ink.
  4. Allow the ink to dry before using or washing.
